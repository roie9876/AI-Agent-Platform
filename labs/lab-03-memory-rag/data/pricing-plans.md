# Acme Corp — Product Pricing & Plans
**Document Version:** 4.0 | **Last Updated:** March 2026 | **Owner:** Product Team

## 1. Plan Overview

| Plan | Price | Users | Storage | Support |
|------|-------|-------|---------|---------|
| **Starter** | $29/month | Up to 5 users | 10 GB | Email only (48h response) |
| **Professional** | $79/month | Up to 25 users | 100 GB | Email + Chat (24h response) |
| **Enterprise** | $199/month | Unlimited users | 1 TB | Priority (4h response) + Dedicated CSM |
| **Enterprise Plus** | Custom pricing | Unlimited | Unlimited | 24/7 phone + SLA guarantee |

All plans are billed monthly. Annual billing available with **20% discount**.

## 2. Add-Ons

| Add-On | Price | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Extra Storage | $5/month per 50 GB | Additional cloud storage |
| API Access | $49/month | REST API with 10,000 calls/month |
| Advanced Analytics | $29/month | Custom dashboards and reports |
| SSO (SAML) | $15/user/month | Enterprise single sign-on |
| Audit Log | Included in Enterprise+ | 90-day activity log retention |

## 3. Free Trial
- **14-day free trial** of the Professional plan
- No credit card required
- Automatic downgrade to Starter (free tier) after trial
- Trial data is preserved for 30 days after downgrade

## 4. Volume Discounts
- 50+ users: **10% discount** on per-user pricing
- 200+ users: **20% discount** on per-user pricing
- 500+ users: **custom pricing** — contact sales@acmecorp.com
- Non-profit organizations: **50% discount** on all plans

## 5. Billing & Payment
- Accepted: Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), wire transfer (Enterprise only)
- Invoices generated on the 1st of each month
- Late payments: 1.5% monthly interest after 30 days overdue
- Currency: USD only (EUR invoicing available for Enterprise+)
