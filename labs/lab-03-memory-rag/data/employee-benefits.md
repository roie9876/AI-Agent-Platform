# Acme Corp — Employee Benefits & Leave Policy
**Document Version:** 5.1 | **Last Updated:** February 2026 | **Owner:** HR Department

## 1. Annual Leave (Vacation)
- All full-time employees receive **22 vacation days** per year
- Part-time employees receive vacation days prorated by hours worked
- Unused vacation days: up to **5 days** can carry over to the next year
- Vacation requests must be submitted **at least 2 weeks** in advance
- Peak periods (Dec 15 - Jan 5, Jul 1-15): maximum 3 employees per team on leave simultaneously

## 2. Sick Leave
- **12 sick days** per year (no carry-over)
- Sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days requires a doctor's note
- Mental health days count as sick days — no documentation needed for single days

## 3. Remote Work Policy
- Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week**
- Must be available on Microsoft Teams during core hours: **10:00 AM - 4:00 PM** (local time)
- Full remote work requires **manager + VP approval** and is granted for up to 6 months
- Equipment: company provides laptop, monitor, and $500 home office stipend (one-time)

## 4. Parental Leave
- Birth parents: **16 weeks** paid leave at full salary
- Non-birth parents: **8 weeks** paid leave at full salary
- Additional 4 weeks unpaid leave available upon request
- Gradual return option: 50% schedule for first 2 weeks back

## 5. Professional Development
- **$3,000/year** professional development budget per employee
- Covers: conferences, courses, certifications, books
- Up to **5 days** paid study leave for certification exams
- Requires manager pre-approval for expenses over $500
