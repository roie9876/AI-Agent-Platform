# Acme Corp — Refund & Returns Policy
**Document Version:** 3.2 | **Last Updated:** January 2026 | **Owner:** Legal Department

## 1. Standard Refund Policy
All customers are eligible for a full refund within **14 calendar days** of purchase. After 14 days, refunds are prorated based on usage.

## 2. Enterprise Customer Refunds
Enterprise customers (contracts above $10,000/year) have extended refund terms:
- Full refund within **30 calendar days** of contract signing
- After 30 days: prorated refund minus a 10% early termination fee
- Annual contracts can be cancelled with 60 days written notice

## 3. Refund Process
1. Submit a refund request via the Support Portal or email support@acmecorp.com
2. Include your order number and reason for the refund
3. Refunds are processed within **5-7 business days** to the original payment method
4. For wire transfers, refunds may take up to 14 business days

## 4. Non-Refundable Items
- Setup fees and onboarding charges
- Custom development work already delivered
- Data migration services once completed
- Third-party license fees passed through to the customer

## 5. Exceptions
- Documented service outages exceeding 4 hours: eligible for SLA credits (see SLA document)
- Security incidents caused by Acme Corp: full refund regardless of timeline
- Beta/preview features: no refunds as these are provided at no charge
